# Account and data deletion - PokeData

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data
for the **PokeData** app, maintained by the project developer (contact below).

## In-app deletion (immediate)

1. Open PokeData and sign in.
2. Go to **Account** (profile).
3. Tap **Delete account** and confirm.
4. Reauthenticate (password or Google, depending on account type).
5. Your account and linked data are deleted immediately.

## Email request (within 30 days)

If you cannot use the app:

1. Email **pokedata.app@gmail.com**.
2. Subject: `Account deletion request - PokeData`.
3. Include the **account email** for the PokeData account to delete.
4. We complete deletion within **30 days** of receiving the request.

## Data deleted

- Firebase Auth account (email, display name, provider links).
- Synced favorites under `users/{uid}/favorites`.
- Local favorites and session on the device after in-app deletion.

## Not account personal data

- Local PokéAPI catalog cache / app assets.
