# Privacy Policy - PokeData

Last updated: July 6, 2026

## 1) Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how PokeData collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data when you use the app.

## 2) Data We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process:

- account data (such as email and display name) when authentication is used;
- app usage and diagnostics data (for stability, analytics, and troubleshooting);
- preferences and local settings (such as language and feature toggles);
- favorites and app-related content linked to your account, when available.

## 3) How We Collect Data

We collect data:

- directly from you (for example, when creating or using an account);
- automatically through app operation and service logs;
- via third-party services that support app features (for example, Firebase/Google services).

## 4) Purposes of Processing

We process data to:

- provide and maintain core app functionality;
- authenticate users and secure accounts;
- sync user-specific features such as favorites;
- improve performance, reliability, and user experience;
- comply with legal obligations and protect against abuse or fraud.

## 5) Legal Bases (LGPD-oriented)

Where Brazilian data protection law applies, processing may be based on:

- performance of a contract or pre-contractual procedures;
- legitimate interest (for security, fraud prevention, app improvement);
- compliance with legal/regulatory obligations;
- consent, where legally required.

## 6) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.
We may share data only with:

- service providers required to operate app features (for example, cloud/auth/analytics infrastructure);
- competent authorities when required by law or legal process;
- successors in case of merger, acquisition, or legal restructuring, subject to safeguards.

## 7) International Transfers

Third-party infrastructure may process data outside your country.
When applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards required by law.

## 8) Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, security, and accounting requirements.

## 9) Security

We adopt commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.
No method is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 10) Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (including LGPD, where applicable), you may request:

- confirmation of processing and access to personal data;
- correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data;
- anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary or excessive data;
- portability, where technically feasible;
- information about data sharing;
- review of decisions made solely by automated processing, when applicable;
- revocation of consent, when processing is consent-based.

## 11) Cookies and Similar Technologies

On web builds, PokeData and third-party providers may use cookies or similar technologies for session handling, functionality, analytics, and security.
You can manage cookies in your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

## 12) Children's Data

PokeData is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.
If you believe a child provided personal data improperly, contact us so we can evaluate and take appropriate action.

## 13) Third-Party Links and Services

The app may reference external links or third-party services (for example, PokéAPI).
Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

## 14) Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
The latest version will be available in the app and/or repository legal assets.

## 15) Contact

For privacy requests or questions, email **pokedata.app@gmail.com**.
